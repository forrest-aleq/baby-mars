### Amanda's Monthly Covenant Compliance Marathon

Amanda Torres arrives at her workstation at 6:45 AM on the third Friday of each month, knowing that the next eight hours will be consumed by one of the most critical and anxiety-inducing processes in StorageCorner's financial management cycle: analyzing debt covenant compliance and expense ratios across seventeen different loan agreements to ensure the company remains within all lender-imposed financial restrictions that could trigger acceleration clauses, additional collateral requirements, or even loan defaults. As Covenant Analyst, Amanda serves as the early warning system that protects StorageCorner from catastrophic covenant violations while providing strategic guidance on operational decisions that impact financial ratios.

Her desk setup reflects the precision required for covenant analysis: dual monitors displaying loan agreement PDFs and complex financial models, printed covenant calculation worksheets with highlighted ratio formulas, calculator for verification calculations, and direct phone lines to relationship managers at seven different lending institutions. Amanda knows that covenant compliance isn't just about historical analysis - it's about forward-looking scenario planning that keeps StorageCorner safely within all financial boundaries while maximizing operational flexibility for growth and acquisitions.

**DSCR Calculation Framework:**

**Covenant Requirements:** Range from 1.10x (construction) to 1.25x (stabilized properties)
**Calculation Period:** Trailing 12 months for stabilized properties, projected for development
