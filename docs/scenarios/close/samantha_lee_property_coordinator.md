### Samantha's Focused Financial Visibility Project

Samantha Lee opens her laptop at 8:30 AM in her shared workspace at StorageCorner's regional operations center, ready to tackle the expense awareness challenge that has emerged as property managers struggle with information overload from QuickBooks reports. As a Property Coordinator responsible for supporting 8 self-storage facilities across Northern California, Samantha has become the go-between for property managers who feel overwhelmed by comprehensive financial reports when they only need to focus on their 8-10 controllable expense categories.

Her role sits at the intersection of finance and operations: translating complex accounting data into actionable information that property managers can actually use to improve their facility performance. Samantha has spent the past 6 months developing filtered reporting approaches that show managers exactly what they control - nothing more, nothing less.
